#  Air Purifier Market Fit Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard developed to evaluate the market potential for launching air purifier products in India. By combining Air Quality Index (AQI), public health, electric vehicle adoption, and population-related datasets, this project identifies high-demand regions, pollution trends, health impacts, and strategic opportunities for AirPure Innovations.

##  Short Description / Purpose

India is home to several of the world's most polluted cities, creating growing concerns around respiratory health and indoor air quality. This dashboard helps AirPure Innovations assess the viability of entering the air purifier market by analyzing pollution severity, disease burden, seasonal AQI trends, and consumer demand indicators.

The project transforms environmental and health data into actionable business insights, enabling data-driven decisions regarding product development, market prioritization, and go-to-market strategy.


##  Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

*  Power BI Desktop – Data visualization and dashboard development<br>
*  Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation<br>
*  DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI calculations and analytical measures<br>
*  Data Modeling – Relationship management across AQI, health, EV, and population datasets<br>
*  File Format – PBIX for development and Power BI Service for deployment<br>

---

##  Data Sources

The analysis combines multiple datasets covering environmental, health, and mobility indicators across India:

* Air Quality Index (AQI) Data
* Pollutant Concentration Data
* Disease & Health Statistics
* Electric Vehicle Adoption Data
* Population & Regional Demographics
* External Market Research and Industry Insights

Analysis period spans primarily from 2022–2025, enabling trend identification and strategic forecasting.

---

#  Features & Highlights

##  Business Problem

AirPure Innovations aims to launch a new air purifier product but needs to determine:

* Which regions have the highest demand potential?
* Which pollutants should the purifier target?
* What health concerns are most closely linked to poor air quality?
* When should marketing campaigns be launched?
* Which product features are essential for market success?

Without a structured analysis of pollution and health trends, market-entry decisions carry significant risk.


##  Goal of the Dashboard

To evaluate India's air purifier market opportunity by:

* Identifying AQI hotspots
* Understanding pollution patterns
* Linking air quality with public health impacts
* Discovering demand triggers
* Supporting product and market-entry decisions


#  Walkthrough of Key Visuals

###  Q1 – Top & Bottom AQI Areas

Analyzes average AQI levels between December 2024 and May 2025.

Key Insight:

* Byrnihat, Delhi, Hajipur, Bahadurgarh, and Gurugram emerge as the highest-risk regions.
* These cities represent priority markets for air purifier adoption.


###  Q2 – Prominent Pollutants in Southern States

Identifies dominant pollutants across South Indian states from 2022 onwards.

Key Insight:

* PM10 and PM2.5 are consistently the most prevalent pollutants.
* Effective particulate filtration should be a core product requirement.

---

###  Q3 – Weekday vs Weekend AQI Comparison

Compares AQI levels across major metro cities.

Key Insight:

* AQI generally improves during weekends.
* Traffic and industrial activity appear to be major contributors to urban pollution.


###  Q4 – Monthly AQI Trends Across States

Evaluates seasonal pollution trends across the top 10 states.

Key Insight:

* November through January consistently experience the worst air quality.
* These months present the strongest opportunity for marketing campaigns and sales initiatives.


###  Q5 – Bengaluru AQI Classification

Categorizes Bengaluru's AQI between March and May 2025.

Key Insight:

* Most days fall under Satisfactory or Moderate categories.
* Bengaluru represents a developing market with long-term growth potential.


###  Q6 – Disease Burden vs AQI

Examines relationships between reported diseases and air quality.

Key Insight:

* States with higher AQI often report increased cases of respiratory illnesses and upper respiratory infections.
* Pollution carries measurable public health consequences, strengthening demand for preventive solutions.


###  Q7 – EV Adoption vs AQI

Compares electric vehicle adoption with air quality improvements.

Key Insight:

* Higher EV adoption does not automatically result in better AQI.
* Air pollution is influenced by multiple factors beyond transportation alone.


#  Strategic Insights & Recommendations

##  Market Prioritization

* Focus initial expansion on Delhi, Gurugram, and Byrnihat.
* Target densely populated regions with consistently poor air quality.
* Develop a city-level risk scoring model combining AQI and population density.


##  Product Development Strategy

Recommended product features:

* PM2.5 Filtration
* PM10 Filtration
* Real-Time AQI Monitoring
* Automatic Air Quality Response Mode
* Mobile App Connectivity
* Smart Filter Replacement Alerts


##  Health-Focused Positioning

* Align campaigns with respiratory health awareness.
* Focus messaging around asthma prevention and family wellness.
* Build partnerships with healthcare professionals and wellness brands.


##  Go-To-Market Strategy

* Launch promotional campaigns during October–January peak pollution season.
* Begin with Tier-1 cities through eCommerce channels.
* Expand into retail channels after validating market demand.


#  Business Impact

This dashboard enables businesses to move beyond assumptions and use environmental, health, and demographic data to evaluate product-market fit. The analysis identifies where demand is likely to emerge, which product features matter most, and how market-entry strategies can be optimized using real-world data.

By connecting AQI trends with public health outcomes and consumer demand indicators, the dashboard provides a strong analytical foundation for strategic decision-making within the air purifier industry.

The Air Purifier Market Fit Analysis Dashboard successfully demonstrates how environmental and public health analytics can be leveraged to identify market opportunities, shape product strategy, and support evidence-based business decisions for a new product launch.
